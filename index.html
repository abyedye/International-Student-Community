Missing home? We make you feel like home.

Prakash Kafle July 22, 2018 44 Comments

Sometimes things doesn’t happen the way you think of. How exciting we feel when we start imagining things in our mind before it even get started but sometimes it do get scary. Bottomline , there is equality in thinking capacity of a brain. Eventually sometimes things are escaping away from us right in front of our eyes but we are not capable enough to hold enough but just letting it go for a good cause is the only option left. Isn’t it irony that you let go something not because you are fed up but just for the sake of someone’s happiness. This line maybe more relatable to the love and feelings but it applies to everyone and everything in the world. Each day someone is sacrificing something, in sake of happiness in coming days. Sadly, People are so much caught on others happiness forgetting about their own. The worst case scenario is even if the person gets seconds of happiness this habit becomes addiction and people can’t help themselves to get out of it. Depression, isolation and cursing own life is the final outcome. Make everyone happy is good thing but it can never be done cause you never know what runs in the person’s mind. The only person you can control is you, and then what’s stopping you to step up to make you happy? There are excuses, but not all the time. You better start making yourself happy, it may be inspiration to someone looking at you. Indirectly you will be making your life better and also, the person who may look at you. It’s upto you whether you search for temporary happiness or the long term happiness.

Winning over the Stressful Environment

Manoj Sapkota June 10, 2020 100 Comments

You will learn only some things are meant for you. You will learn to let go. You will cry, you will be stressed, broken and empty but it will nourish inside you. Just like water makes plants grow better, the tears will flow inside you and make you stronger increasing your capacity to bear the situation. Get relaxed, think it’s you that was suppose to beat this situation and get victory over it. Don’t say why me , say why not me ? Give whatever you can, learn to be fearless. Let go everything and the good things will come to you. It’s okay to make people happy but it’s even better you start from yourself.
